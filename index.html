Nick Jones

Durham, NC - Designer & Prototyper Nick Jones is an ever-student of Design, an adventurer, a husband and a father of 4. His life is peppered with moments of uncertainty, awesome leaps of faith, incredible discoveries. As you'll see, pursuing purpose, not a plan, is always the priority with Nick.

Future Father

We spend a lot of hours away from our kids, staring at devices, building things that our friends and clients will think are cool. Future Father is our chance to make something meaningful. To focus what we are good at, creative uses of technology, on something we care deeply about.

The Experiment - Design and Technology Studio

We are a design and technology studio trying to figure out what exactly we are and who we want to work with. To keep quality high and relationships right, we are being selective. Our dance card is not full. We're beginning to partner with brands and startups on the prototyping, design and marketing of early stage products.
